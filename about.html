Who Am I?

My name is Isha Whitney J, a Cyber Security student from Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

I chose Cyber Security because it is one of the few disciplines that refuses stagnation. Every technological breakthrough introduces new opportunities, new vulnerabilities, and new questions waiting to be explored.

Rather than seeking a profession that becomes predictable, I wanted a field capable of evolving continuously throughout my lifetime. Cyber Security represents exactly that challenge.
